Hackers?!?!!

May 31st, 2009

Hackers are people who modify the software or hardware of their computer system including building, rebuilding, modifying and creating software and electronic hardware either to make it better, faster, give added features or to make it do something it was never intended to do.

Crackers are people who illegally break in to computer systems.

This site is targetted at hobbyist hackers - people who just want to make technology do cool stuff, often stuff it was never intended to do (such as turning on a light using a Wii remote…)

About

SoutHACKton is a not-for-profit community of makers, tinkerers and artists. We have regular meetings - experience is unnecessary! Find out more or join our mailing list.

Our aim is to establish and maintain a well-equiped space where members can share skills and tools to allow people of all abilities to create something amazing, regardless of experience, whether it be: craft, electronics, wood- and metal-work, knitting, programming, modification (a.k.a. hacking), robotics, home automation or something else.
